FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Bascode.

How do I place an order?
Simply click the "Order Now" button, fill in your details (name, email, phone, country, and project description), upload any reference files, and submit. You'll receive an Order ID instantly — no registration required.
How can I track my order?
Use the "Track Order" feature in the navigation menu. Enter your Order ID and phone number to see real-time updates on your project's status, from "Order Received" to "Completed."
What is the typical delivery time?
Average delivery time is 24 hours for standard projects. Complex projects may take 48–72 hours. Specific timelines are provided when you place your order based on the service type and scope.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDT), and bank transfers for enterprise clients. Payment is processed securely through encrypted channels.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes! Every project includes up to 3 free revisions. We're committed to delivering results that exceed your expectations. Additional revisions can be arranged as needed.
